Beans

Hots beans in a bowl

This is a recipe passed down from generation to generation. Its easy to make and only takes 30 mins! The most important part of this recipe is to make sure you keep an eye on it while it slowly simmers : ) This is good for when you have a cold or just want a nice hearty meal to get you through the day.

Well thats enough talking! Lets get right to what we need to make this awesome meal! You will need a pot and a spoon...i know right...so little is needed!!!

Ingredients

  • Beans
  • Onions
  • Adobo
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  1. Heat pan to 450
  2. Chop whole onion and place into pan
  3. Open can of beans and drain water
  4. Once onions are golden brown, pour the beans into pan
  5. Let it simmer for 1 hour and then eat!
